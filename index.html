A fun toddler coloring training on classroom

A fun toddler coloring training in the classroom is a delightful activity that sparks creativity. The teacher starts by distributing sheets with simple images like animals, flowers, or shapes. Toddlers are given crayons in vibrant colors to explore their imagination.

The little ones giggle with excitement as they pick their favorite colors. Some carefully try to color within the lines, while others happily scribble in their unique style.
